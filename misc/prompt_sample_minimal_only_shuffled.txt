Boil and stir 5 minutes more. Take off heat. Let stand until firm, about 30 minutes. In a heavy 2-quart saucepan, mix brown sugar, nuts, evaporated milk and butter or margarine. Stir in vanilla and cereal; mix well. Using 2 teaspoons, drop and shape into 30 clusters on wax paper. Stir over medium heat until mixture bubbles all over top.
