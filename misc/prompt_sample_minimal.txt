Make a few chocolate curls for decoration then break the rest of the chocolate into squares& melt with the maderia in a heatproof bowl over simmering water. Spoon the mixture into 6 small ramekin dishes& chill until set. Remove from heat& add the butter a few pieces at a time, stirring until melted& smooth. Then evenly fold in the rest. Stir in 1 tablespoon of the whites into the chestnut mixture to lighten it. Beat the egg whites in another bowl until stiff. Mix until smooth. Beat the egg yolks quickly into the mixture, then beat in the chestnut puree, a little at a time, making sure each addition is absorbed before you add the next.
